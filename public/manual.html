Developer Tools

The current build includes developer tools that you may accidentally interact with on the keyboard:

  • [E] will toggle the level editor on/off. The level editor allows free movement which can be useful if you encounter a bug and get stuck in a wall or if you just like to explore out of bounds or skip puzzles. Be warned that turning on the level editor may hurt the immersion of the game for you.
  • [R] will reset the current area as if you left and returned to it. Mostly useful if you want to restart a puzzle.
  • [SHIFT]+[R] will clear most progress flags for the current dungeon which will respawn all enemies, bosses, close opened chests and doors, and reset switches and other puzzle elements.
  • [CTRL]+[K] will defeat all enemies including bosses in the current super tile.
  • Opening the context menu (right click, or control click on macs) will open a menu that allows you to either teleport to certain locations, or test certain states in the game. These options will overwrite your current save file, so please use them carefully

Title Controls

X-Box
  • Use D-pad or left analog stick to move the cursor
  • Use [start], [A] or [B] to select an option
Keyboard
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move the cursor
  • Use [ENTER], [SPACE] or [SHIFT] to select an option

Field Controls

X-Box
  • Use D-pad or left analog stick to move your character
  • Use [start] to open your inventory
  • [A] Primary Weapon (once obtained)
  • [B] Interact (open, read, talk, pick up, grab)
  • [X]/[Y] Equiped Tools
  • [L2] Mist Roll (once obtained)
  • [R2] Meditate (once obtained)
  • [L1]/[R1] Switch to previous/next element (once obtained)
  • Use [start], [A] or [B] to advance text or confirm text options
Keyboard
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Use [ENTER] to open your inventory
  • [SPACE] Primary Weapon (once obtained)
  • [SHIFT] Interact (open, read, talk, pick up, grab)
  • [C]/[V] Equiped Tools
  • [Z] Mist Roll (once obtained)
  • [X] Meditate (once obtained)
  • Use [ENTER], [SPACE] or [SHIFT] to advance text or confirm text options

Inventory Controls

X-Box
  • Use D-pad or left analog stick to move your cursor
  • [A]/[B] to select the help option, boots, or element under your cursor
  • [X]/[Y] to assign the tool under your cursor to the [X]/[Y] button
Keyboard
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your cursor
  • [SPACE]/[SHIFT] to select the help option, boots, or element under your cursor
  • [C]/[V] to assign the tool under your cursor to the [C]/[V] key

Exploration Tips

  • You can interact with bushes to pick them up and throw them
  • Some objects can be pushed or pulled if you hold the interact button to grab them and then move.
